  • About Me

    Accept no imitations

    Wendy Suarez takes joy in sharing her unique gifts as she continues to discover and express who she is as an artist. She was born on December 13, 1981 in New Bedford, Massachusetts and raised in New Jersey where she lives as of current. She spent the majority of her childhood growing up in Bayonne with her family and friends while developing her artistic persona. There, she went to Bayonne High School and later attended the New Jersey City University for Illustration. Born with the natural ability to draw, she experimented with different styles and techniques of art to develop her own. Her work illustrates a dominant interest in exploring the themes/genres of comic art, cartoons, fantasy, dreams, stylized portraits, and erotic art. She is also heavily driven and influenced by select styles of music that inspire a portion of her work. Her intent is to keep in tune with her Spirit, grow as an artist, allow new ideas to flow, explore creative boundaries, develop her skill level, and most importantly, keep the burning desire to Create alive.

    Experienced and Accomplished

    Wendy has made appearances and exhibited in various art shows including the Art Factory, QXT’s Night Club and Newark Comic Con. She was also hired by the WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment) to design a t-shirt logo for female wrestler, ‘Lita’ in 2001.

    She has also made showing outside in NJ - such as in California and most recently at the NYC Comicon artist gallery, where she sold many of her artworks and met many clients.

    She is avaliable in a 24/7 basis - art never sleeps and its a commodity. She is constantly hustling and always looking foward to working with new and exciting clients

    At the vanguard of Art

    Originally trained in traditional media - charcoal, graphite, watercolors, acrylic and gouche - This artist never chooses to settle for one media and she is constantly searching for ways to bring her art using the latest technological media avaliable - be it Painter, Adobe CC, or Zbrush - Art never ceases to flourish from her mind into reality
